Visual & Web Design

Where stays define your story.

A website for a boutique Glasgow hotel that blends elegance and hospitality in every detail where finding and reserving a room feels as considered as the stay itself.

RoleVisual & Web Designer
ToolsFigma

The Brief

A hotel for adventure travellers, built to convert.

Haus is a new premier hotel in Glasgow's city centre, built for a niche audience of adventure travellers. The brief: a website that communicates the brand's adventurous, sophisticated identity, makes booking effortless across devices, and turns that visual impression into reservations.

Proposed Solution

Five features built for explorers

Adventure Concierge

Adventure Packages

Gear Storage

User-Centric Navigation

Seamless Booking

Local Discovery

Process

Research, distilled to what mattered.

Competitor benchmarking showed most boutique-hotel sites bury booking behind clutter so personas, empathy mapping and wireframe iterations all pushed toward one goal: a single, unhurried path from browsing to booking.

Deliver · Design System

Tactile luxury, translated into pixels.

Warm neutrals, a confident sans-serif and a single accent Burnt Red used with restraint.
