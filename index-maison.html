Nº IIAbout

A maker who fell
for experiences.

I spent eight years in fashion designing, merchandising and directing brand work where I learned that taste is a discipline: composition, restraint, and an obsession with the final detail.

Today I bring that trained eye to UX and product design. I work end-to-end from research and strategy to polished, tested interfaces and I still draw and paint by hand, which keeps my sense of craft honest.

Nº IIIHow I Work

A Double Diamond,
research to delivery

Diverging to explore the problem widely, then converging on a focused, tested solution.

I

Discover

Research into clients, competitors and users to uncover real, unmet needs.

II

Define

Synthesis into empathy maps, personas and a single sharp problem.

III

Develop

Sketches, flows and wireframes iterated and tested with real users.

IV

Deliver

A cohesive system and a polished, high-fidelity prototype.
