02 — UX / UI · Mobile App

Finding your way in Glasgow.

Glasgo turns an unfamiliar city into somewhere that feels like home — helping the 50,000+ students who arrive in Glasgow each year discover, save and explore with confidence.

Prototype ↗
RoleUX / UI Designer
MethodDouble Diamond
BriefSelf-directed
ToolsFigma

The Problem

A new city that's hard to call home.

New students often struggle with the city's layout, social venues and affordable options — which slows their integration in those crucial first weeks. Glasgo answers this with a single, accessible guide that connects students to Glasgow's vibrant community.

Navigation

An unfamiliar layout makes everyday journeys feel daunting.

Belonging

Without local connections, the city stays unfamiliar for too long.

Affordability

Hard to find student-friendly deals scattered across the city.

Slow integration

The crucial first weeks pass before students feel settled.

Discovery

Common thoughts on moving to a new city.

Interviews with new and international students surfaced the same anxieties again and again.

"A new city feels overwhelming at first." "I don't know where to find good deals." "I wish I knew where to meet people." "Where do I even start exploring?"

The Solution

One companion for student city life.

Discover, save, explore and connect — with a reason to keep coming back.

Interactive map

Locate popular spots across Glasgow at a glance.

Curated picks

Social venues, dining and cultural attractions.

Student offers

Exclusive discounts that help newcomers save.

Real-time updates

Stay informed about the latest events.

Gamification

Earn rewards for exploring the city.

Accessibility

Clear navigation and high-contrast visuals.

Research Competitor analysis

I compared city, student and discovery apps to find where Glasgo could add unique value — combining trustworthy curation with a reason to return.

Journey Maps Empathy maps, personas & POV

From insight to a focused brief — the emotional core: the need to belong, fast.

Kano and MoSCoW models separated must-have functionality from optional enhancements — keeping Glasgo focused on a seamless, engaging student experience.

Wireframes & Iterations Sketches, IA & mid-fi

Many ideas per screen, then the strongest refined into wireframes.

Information architecture keeps discovery, deals, gamification and community easy to navigate from day one.

Outcome

Designing for belonging, not just utility.

The gamified check-in turned a practical map into a reason to explore, and accessibility was a first-class feature, not an afterthought. Glasgo taught me how much emotion drives adoption in a brand-new environment.

"It would have made the city feel like home so much faster."— International student, Glasgow
