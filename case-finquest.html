01 Gamified Finance App

Making finance fun to learn.

FinQuest turns financial literacy into a game helping young adults build money confidence through personalised, interactive learning.

Prototype ↗
RoleUX / UI Designer
MethodDouble Diamond
ToolsFigma

The Problem

Money is everywhere but financial confidence isn't.

Only one in three adults worldwide grasp basic financial principles. Traditional education is dense and dull, so this digital-native generation switches off and the knowledge never sticks.

Saving

No clear starting point or goal to work toward.

Budgeting

Tracking spending feels tedious, so it gets skipped.

Credit

Scores and debt feel confusing and high-stakes.

Investing

Jargon-heavy and intimidating for first-timers.

Discovery

Common thoughts on money.

Interviews and an empathy map surfaced the same tension again and again people want to learn about money, but every resource feels heavy, serious and easy to abandon.

"Finance seems complex and serious." "I wish learning about money was more fun." "I can't stay interested in long, detailed articles." "Where do I even start?"

The Solution

Six features that make finance approachable.

Every feature reframes a chore as a rewarding moment.

Personalised learning

Tailored content for a unique journey.

Interactive flashcards

Bite-sized cards, easy to grasp.

Leaderboard

Friendly competition that motivates.

Trivia quizzes

Fun tests that reinforce learning.

Achievement badges

Rewards that keep users engaged.

Financial news

Timely updates, always in view.
