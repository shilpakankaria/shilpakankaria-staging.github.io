04 UX / UI · Mobile App

Every document, organised.

MigrantOS tracks visas, documents and travel logs, with checklists and reminders for what's next — solo or as a family.

MigrantOS
Prototype ↗
RoleUX / UI Designer

The Problem

Moving country means paperwork chaos.

Four pressure points hit every relocating family at once.

Scattered documents

Passports, visas and BRP cards live across a dozen apps and folders.

Missed deadlines

Visa renewals and expiries slip by with no central reminder.

A whole family

Every member has their own documents, dates and conditions.

Unfamiliar systems

A new country brings a rulebook no one hands you.

Discovery

What families say about moving abroad.

Research surfaced the same pressure points again and again — overwhelm, disorganisation, and the fear of missing something important.

"I had documents in five different places and still missed a renewal." "My husband's documents, my documents, the kids' — it's a full-time job." "I didn't know my BRP was expiring until it was too late." "There's nowhere to see everything the family needs in one place."

The Solution

Six features that calm a chaotic transition.

Every feature removes one category of stress from the family's migration journey.

Document Vault

Every document for every family member, searchable and categorised.

Smart Reminders

Expiry alerts and renewal nudges, per family member.

Arrival Checklist

A curated to-do covering every step of settling in.

Household Profiles

Manage the whole family from one device.

Migration Timeline

A visual record of key milestones on the journey.

Travel Log

Record trips abroad to track days out for visa compliance.

Process

Human-centred design, four stages.

From empathy to a sharp problem statement, validated in design and refined through testing.

01

Empathise

Conversations with migrant families to find where stress builds.

02

Define

Personas and a sharp problem statement for the lead applicant.

03

Design

User flows, wireframes and high-fidelity screens on a full design system.

04

Test

Walkthroughs with target users to validate and refine.
